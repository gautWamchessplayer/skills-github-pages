Facts about water pollution.

  1. Water pollution occurs when harmful substances contaminate water, degrading its quality and rendering it toxic to humans and the environment.
  2. Causes of water pollution include chemicals and waste dumping.
  3. Effects of water pollution include sickness or death of organic life.
  4. Types of water pollution include surface, ground, nutrient, and oil spill pollution.
  5. The Mississippi River is one of the most polluted water bodies in the US.
  6. Solutions to water pollution include conservation and treatment.
