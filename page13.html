Facts about air pollution.

  1. Air pollution costs human health. 7 million people die yearly as a result of air pollution.
  2. Over 99% of the Earth's population breathes polluted air daily.
  3. More than 10 percent of deaths worldwide are attributable to air pollution-related disease.
  4. Air pollution cuts life expectancy by up to 3.5 years.
  5. Low and middle-income countries have the highest rates of air pollution-related deaths.
  6. In children, air pollution is associated with low birth weight, asthma, childhood cancers, obesity, poor lung development, and autism, among other health defects.
